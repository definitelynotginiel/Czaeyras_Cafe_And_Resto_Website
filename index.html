WHY US

Czaeyra's Café & Resto is a very relaxing place where you can have a sip of your favorite Milktea, Coffee or Frappé. We also serve Burgers, Fries, Ramens and Chicken Wings. You can choose from a variety of flavors available in our menu.

We provide affordable meals, ideal for students and for anyone looking for budget friendly meals! Located near Primark, Sub Urban, Rodriguez, Rizal, an area where many people pass by on a daily basis. Several schools are also located near our café, making it a convenient spot for students.
