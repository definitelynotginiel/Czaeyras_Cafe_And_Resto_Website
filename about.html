Our Story

Czaeyra’s Cafe and Resto was first established in the year 2020, at the beginning of the Covid 19 pandemic in the Philippines and was first known as "CVWolf Cafe". This small cafe and restaurant was founded by the couple, Mr. Rhayon Joseph Fernandez and Mrs. Czarina Atienza.

We started our business in a small space in Eastwood Residences, Rodriguez Rizal. The "Wolf" in the name of our Cafe comes from our business of breeding Alaskan Malamute dogs, which we are very passionate about.

Some of our Alaskan Malamute dogs are inside of the CVWolf Cafe, they were there to entertain the customers while dining in and also to showcase them for selling. CVWolf is more than just a cafe and resto; it is a place where friendships are forged, families gather, and memories are made.

CVWolf Cafe was also featured in a vlog by the social media influencer "Ewic Mukbang". They made a Mukbang vlog for the cafe, showcasing our delicious food and the warm atmosphere of our cafe. Watch here

However, our business faced financial challenges and bankruptcy, eventually leading to the closing of CVWolf Cafe. But in the year 2025, after recovering from financial problems, we are finally able to open our cafe and restaurant again, this time with a new name: "Czaeyra's Cafe and Resto".

Although the showcasing of Alaskan Malamute dogs is no longer in our new store, we sought to revive the good ambience and delicious food that CVWolf Cafe was known for. We are now located in Double L, San Jose, Rodriguez Rizal, Philippines. Czaeyra's Cafe and Resto was first opened on January 18, 2025. Its nearest landmark is Primark, Double L Branch.

About the Founders

As a newly opened café and restaurant in the area, we aim to make Czaeyra's Cafe and Resto well-known in Rodriguez, Rizal—and eventually, recognized by a wider audience. Whether you're a resident of Rodriguez or a tourist visiting Montalban, we warmly invite you to drop by our small café and restaurant to enjoy quality food, professionally prepared by our dedicated staff!

We strongly believe that businesses that started from humble beginnings operated with dedication and passion would grow into a successful and wealthy business. Come to our small Cafe and Resto and help us fulfill our vision!
